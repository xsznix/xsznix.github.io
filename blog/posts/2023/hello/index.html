Hello!

Welcome to my new blog! If, for some reason, you’re interested in what I have to say, you’re in the right place.

Now is the perfect time to get started with a blog as a web frontend developer. The next generation of JavaScript tooling is more exciting than ever for content-heavy websites, having shed off the burden of the single page application mindset.

While I can’t claim that my writings are nearly as iconic as any of these, I want to shout out the following websites for the inspiration they gave me:

  • Matthew Butterick’s Practical Typography, which taught me everything most people need to know about how to make text look good
  • Bartosz Ciechanowski’s blog, especially his post about mechanical watches, as a great demonstration of how interactivity enhances an explanation of a complex topic
  • Chad Mazzola’s Beautiful Web Type, which led me to the wonderful font family Alegreya by Huerta Tipográfica
